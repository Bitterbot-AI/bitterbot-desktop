Quinoa and black bean salad

Ingredients:

- 1 cup quinoa
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons lime juice

Instructions:

1. Rinse the quinoa in a fine mesh strainer and place it in a medium saucepan with 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Cook for about 18-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the water has been absorbed.
2. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and transfer it to a large bowl to cool.
3. Once the quinoa is cool, add the black beans, red bell pepper, cilantro, and lime juice. Stir well to combine.
4. Serve the salad cold or at room temperature.

Spaghetti Carbonara with a twist

Ingredients:

- 8 oz spaghetti
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup frozen peas
- 2 cloves of garlic

Instructions:

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. While the spaghetti cooks, in a small bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of black pepper.
3. In a pan, heat the olive oil and gently fry the garlic until soft. Then add the frozen peas and cook for a couple of minutes.
4. Reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water, then drain spaghetti.
5. Add the spaghetti to the pan with the garlic and peas, and take off the heat.
6. Pour the egg mixture over the spaghetti and toss everything together, adding a splash of pasta cooking water if it feels too thick.
7. Serve immediately, with extra grated Parmesan cheese on top.

This dish is a classic carbonara with a twist, adding the peas to it, it will give a nice green color and a good source of vitamin C and K. The Parmesan cheese adds a nice nutty flavor and richness, the egg makes it creamy and spaghetti gives a good bite. The garlic gives a nice aroma and flavor.

Roasted red pepper and tomato sauce

Ingredients:

- 2 red bell peppers, roasted, peeled and seeded
- 2 cups of cherry tomatoes
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Cut the tomatoes in half, place them on a baking sheet, add the garlic cloves and drizzle with olive oil. Roast for 25 minutes or until the tomatoes are softened.
2. Place the roasted red peppers, tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil in a blender and puree until smooth.

Creamy Avocado Sauce

Ingredients:

- 1 ripe avocado
- 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 cloves of garlic
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Peel and pit the avocado and place it in a blender or food processor.
2. Add Greek yogurt, lime juice, garlic, salt and pepper, and blend until smooth.
3. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
4. Use the sauce as a dip, dressing for salad or spread on sandwiches.
