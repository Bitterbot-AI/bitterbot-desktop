**User:** Can you suggest a cesar salad recipe

**Assistant:** Certainly! Here is a classic Caesar salad recipe:

Ingredients:

- 1 large head of romaine lettuce, washed and torn into bite-sized pieces
- 1/2 cup of homemade Caesar dressing (recipe below)
- 1 cup of croutons (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup of shaved parmesan cheese
- 1/4 tsp of black pepper

For the Caesar dressing:

- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 anchovy fillets, finely minced (optional)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tbsp of freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 1 tsp of Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp of Worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 cup of olive oil
- 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a small bowl, whisk together the garlic, anchovy fillets (if using), egg yolk, lemon juice, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while continuously whisking the mixture until it is fully emulsified.
3. Stir in the parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. In a large salad bowl, combine the lettuce, croutons, and Caesar dressing. Toss until the lettuce is evenly coated with the dressing.
5. Sprinkle the shaved parmesan cheese on top and finish with a sprinkle of black pepper.
6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Note: If you prefer a vegetarian version of Caesar salad, you can omit the anchovy fillets from the dressing recipe and use a vegetarian Worcestershire sauce.
